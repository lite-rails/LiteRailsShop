About

LiteRails was established in 2020 at the beginning of the Covid-19 Pandemic. They are a Canadian Company founded by Craig Monteith, Welder and Fabricator with over 10 years experience; that supplies custom rails for skaters at home. Additionally LiteRails is immersed in the skater community; creating and donating rails for skateparks, competitions and D.I.Y. spots.
