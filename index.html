About

LiteRails was established in 2020. They are a Canadian Company founded by Craig Monteith, Welder / Fabricator with over 10 years experience and Social Media Conglomerate; that supplies custom rails for skaters at home. Additionally, LiteRails is immersed in the skater community; creating and donating rails for skateparks, competitions and D.I.Y. spots.
